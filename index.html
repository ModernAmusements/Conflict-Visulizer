Understanding the Historical Tension

A Timeline of the Israel-Hamas Conflict (1900-2025)

An educational resource exploring the complex historical events that shaped this ongoing conflict

This timeline aims to provide a comprehensive overview of the key historical events that have contributed to the ongoing tension between Israel and Hamas. Understanding this complex history requires examining multiple perspectives and acknowledging the deep-rooted issues on both sides.

Understanding Different Perspectives

Israeli Perspective

Key concerns include security, recognition of Israel's right to exist, and protection from terrorism.

Palestinian Perspective

Key issues include occupation, settlement expansion, right of return, and self-determination.

Hamas Perspective

Founded in 1987, Hamas combines political and military resistance against Israeli occupation.

This educational timeline is designed to promote understanding of complex historical events. It aims to present facts objectively while acknowledging the suffering and perspectives of all peoples involved.
